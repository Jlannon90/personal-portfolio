Process

Ideation

Identify the intended audience of the project, and research that community. Identify the needs of the client, and research their community. Make note of where those points intersect and diverge. Collaborate with the client and my peers to arrive at a list of necessary features and the projects general theme.

Design

Sketch and wireframe layouts that are accessible and aesthetically pleasing for the user. Decided on a layout and color scheme that are appropriate for the theme of the project and compliment it's purpose. Consider the user experience on multiple viewports and ensure design is reponsive and optimized for each.

Tools

Code finalized design using a combination of HTML(5), CSS(3), and Javascript (with jQuery). For front-end I prefer using SASS and coding from scratch or using Neat's grid system. Other frameworks I am familiar with include Bootstrap, Materialize, and Foundation.
